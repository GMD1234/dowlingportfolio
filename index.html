About

A little context

I'm a CAPM-certified project manager working in professional sports. Currently on the Baseball Systems team at the Tampa Bay Rays, where I help prioritize and deliver projects for our analytics hub — the platform that Baseball Operations relies on for scouting, player development, and decision-making.

Before the Rays, I spent nearly four years at the WTA (Women's Tennis Association), where I managed player rankings for 1,000+ athletes and led a major research initiative tracking down 240+ former players worldwide for the WTA Legacy Fund.

I'm currently pursuing my PMP and Scrum Master certifications. Georgetown MPS in Sports Industry Management, BA from Lake Forest College — where I was a four-year varsity tennis player.
